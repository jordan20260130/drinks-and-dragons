## Cantrips

### Fire Bolt
*Evocation cantrip*

Hurl a mote of fire at a creature or object within range. Make a ranged spell attack. On a hit, the target takes **2d10 fire damage**. Flammable objects not worn or carried ignite.

### Mage Hand
*Conjuration cantrip*

A spectral floating hand appears within range, lasting until dismissed or it moves more than 30 ft away. Use your action to control it and move it up to 30 ft.

---

## Spells

### 1st Level

#### Mage Armor
*Abjuration · 8 hours*

Touch a willing creature not wearing armor. Its base AC becomes **13 + DEX modifier** until it dons armor or you dismiss the spell.

---

### 2nd Level

#### Magic Missile
*Evocation*

Create four glowing darts of magical force. Each dart hits a creature of your choice within 120 ft, dealing **1d4 + 1 force damage** (+ 5 from Empowered Evocation). Darts strike simultaneously.

#### Dragon's Breath
*Transmutation · Concentration, 1 minute*

Touch a willing creature, granting it the power to exhale energy. Choose: acid, cold, fire, lightning, or poison. As an action, the creature exhales a 15-foot cone. Each creature in the area makes a **DC 17 DEX save**, taking **3d6 damage** on a failure (half on success).

---

### 3rd Level

#### Counterspell
*Abjuration · Reaction*

Interrupt a creature casting a spell:
- **3rd level or lower:** Fails automatically
- **4th level or higher:** Make an INT check, DC = 10 + spell level

---

### 4th Level

#### Fireball
*Evocation*

A 20-foot radius explosion at a point within range. Each creature makes a **DEX save**:
- **Failed:** 9d6 + 5 fire damage
- **Success:** Half damage

Fire spreads around corners and ignites unattended flammable objects.

#### Dimension Door
*Conjuration*

Teleport yourself and up to one willing creature to a destination within 500 ft that you can see, visualize, or describe. Arriving in an occupied space deals **4d6 force damage** to each creature and the spell fails.

---

### 5th Level

#### Bigby's Hand
*Evocation · Concentration*

Create a Large hand (55 HP, 20 AC). As a **bonus action**, move it up to 60 ft and choose:

| Option           | Effect                                      |
|------------------|---------------------------------------------|
| Clenched Fist    | +9 to hit, 4d8 force damage                 |
| Forceful Hand    | Contest to push target up to 50 ft          |
| Interposing Hand | Half cover (+2 AC and DEX saves)            |

---

## Racial Traits (Fire Genasi)

### Darkvision
See in dim light within 60 ft as bright light, and darkness as dim light (shades of gray).

### Fire Resistance
Resistance to fire damage (half damage).

---

## Class Features (Evocation Wizard)

### Arcane Recovery
*1/day after short rest*

Recover spell slots totaling up to **5 combined levels**.

### Sculpt Spells
When casting an evocation spell, choose up to **1 + spell level** creatures you can see. They automatically succeed on saves and take no damage from effects that deal half on a success.

### Empowered Evocation
Add **+5** (INT modifier) to damage rolls of evocation spells.
